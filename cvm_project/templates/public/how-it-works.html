How it works

We provide tools for measuring political civility, and apply them to lots of data in real time. We are fair, transparent, accurate, and relevant.

Quick links

Goals

  1. Hold politicians and newsmakers accountable for what they say.
  2. Promote constructive public discussion about civility.
  3. Share tools and data to encourage research on communication.

Philosophy

Civility starts with accountability. Accountability starts with clear standards. Clear standards start with accurate measurement.

Not-so-secret sauce

We combine content analysis, crowdsourcing, and state-of-the-art algorithms to measure civility with high accuracy at large scale. This is computational social science at its best.

Due credit

This prototype was built using using cyclone and mongodb. Responsive layouts are by Twitter bootstrap, the bootswatch unity theme, and the jquery masonry plugin. Graphs are generated in d3. Icons are provided by the noun project.

Data sources

We constantly scan the web for new political content. Our system combines human and artificial intelligence to rate the civility of everything that gets said.
